How it works
1
Add the extension to Chrome
Free install from the Chrome Web Store. Currently under review — sign up above to get notified.
2
Log into Discogs as usual
The extension uses your existing Discogs session — no passwords stored, nothing new to configure.
3
Hit Sync in Wax Digger
Open Wax Digger → Discover → Discogs tab and press Sync. The extension pulls live prices from your wantlist marketplace pages in the background.
4
Build your cheapest cart
The Cart Optimizer combines all Discogs sellers with our 12 stores to find the best combination — fewest orders, lowest total including shipping.
